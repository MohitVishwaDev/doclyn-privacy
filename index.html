Doclyn Vault Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Doclyn ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Doclyn mobile application.

1. Overview

Doclyn is designed as a privacy-first secure document vault. Your data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell your personal documents.

2. Information We Collect

Doclyn does not collect personal data or vault content on external servers.

  • All documents are encrypted
  • Data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your vault

3. Device Permissions

Doclyn may request the following permissions strictly for app functionality:

  • Storage: To save and retrieve your encrypted documents.
  • Biometric Authentication: To enable fingerprint or face unlock features.
  • Camera: To scan or capture documents if you use scanning features.

These permissions are used only for functionality and not for data collection.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your master password.
  • We do not upload your documents to any server.
  • We do not sell or share personal data.
  • We do not track your vault contents.
  • We do not use third-party analytics to access your private documents.

5. Security

Doclyn uses strong encryption methods to protect your stored data. Your master password is never stored in plain text.

If you forget your master password, your encrypted data cannot be recovered. This ensures maximum protection and privacy.

6. Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the application. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data may permanently delete stored content.

7. Children's Privacy

Doclyn is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Third-Party Services

Doclyn does not share your vault data with third parties. Any biometric authentication is processed securely by your device’s operating system.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
